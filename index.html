Interesting Facts about Camels

  • There Are Three Species of Camels
  • Camels can live for about 40 years
  • They can go for weeks without drinking water

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica
  • Cicadas can't eat, getting nutrients from tree roots
  • Some cicadas could explode with fungus

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears
  • They spend a lot of their day eating

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals

Interesting Facts about Kodiak Bears

  • Kodiak bears are the largest bears in the world
  • Kodiak bears live exclusively on the islands in the Kodiak Archipelago in southwest Alaska.
  • They have been isolated on the Kodiak Archipelago since at least the last ice age
